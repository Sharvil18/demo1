Maitake Mushroom


Maitake Mushrooms with feathered edges and an earthy, peppery flavour.

While have a savory taste, the mushroom is also considered to have health benefits.

Saute in a japanese sauce to give it a traditional flavour.




Ingredients -

  • Maitake Mushrooms
  • Sesame Oil
  • Garlic Cloves
  • Soy Sauce
  • Salt
  • Siracha
  • Chives
